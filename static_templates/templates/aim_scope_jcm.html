Aim and Scope

Karpagam JCM (KJCM) aims to publish scholarly research articles on contemporary issues in Commerce and Management.

It offers an opportunity for Academicians, Industrialists, Research Scholars and Students of Commerce and Management to publish their research work.

All the articles will be scrutinized by a Panel of Experts followed by plagiarism verification employing Turnitin software and will be published after carrying out Linguistic Correction.
