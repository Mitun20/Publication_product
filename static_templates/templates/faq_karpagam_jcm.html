Frequently Asked Questions - JCM

Is Karpagam JCM peer-reviewed?

Yes, Karpagam JCM is a single-blind review process. Selected papers forwarded to panel of experts to assess the quality. The Editor’s decision is final.

What types of paper are published by JCS?

Karpagam JCM publishes original Research Papers, Case Studies, Literature Reviews and Conceptual Framework.

Whether Karpagam JCM has ISSN?

No, presently does not have ISSN. But, Application is in process for receipt of ISSN.

Is there a print version of the journal?

No, Karpagam JCM publishes articles only on online.

Can I submit more than one paper for the same issue?

Yes, you can submit more than one paper at a time.

When should I submit my papers? Are there any deadlines?

There is no deadline for paper submission, so you can submit your manuscript at any time. As long as the submitted paper is peer-reviewed and accepted successfully, it would be published in the upcoming issue.

How to submit a paper?

All papers are to be forwarded to the Editor E Mail address.

Can I send only abstract for review?

No, author must send full length paper for review process.

Do I need to submit the copyright form along with the manuscript?

Copyright forms shall be accepted to The Editor, Karpagam JCM only if the article is accepted for publication.

When will I receive an acknowledgment email for my submission?

Paper submissions are normally acknowledged within 12 to 24 hrs.

How many authors may be included in a paper?

Maximum 03 authors may be included in a paper.

Is it compulsory to submit papers in the given journal template?

Yes, authors are advised to submit their papers as per paper template format in MS Word.
