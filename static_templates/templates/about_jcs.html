About JCS ( Journal of Computer Science)

The JCS ISSN: XXXX – XXXX (Print), XXXX – XXXX (Online) is a bimonthly journal in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. This journal is started in the year 2005. It seeks to deliver advanced information with the aim of fostering Information Technology, building insights and enabling individual as well as collective advancements in research.

Title Change: Journal of Computer Science (ISSN: XXXX-XXXX Print) continued as JCS (ISSN: XXXX – XXXX Print) from 2020

The editorial board is a mélange of eminent personalities from prominent institutions and Industry. The journal is a platform to exhibit the skills of research scholars, teaching faculty, industrialists and professionals, and also publish their research work. It enables them to get acquainted with the various resources in the field.
