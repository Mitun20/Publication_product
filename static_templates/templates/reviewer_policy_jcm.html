Journal of Electonic and Communication Engineering

  • Journal of Electonic and Communication Engineering will publish both theoretical and analytical papers having high impact towards social contribution.
  • All the papers submitted to Journal of Electonic and Communication Engineering forwarded to Editorial Board. The board will strictly scrutinize the article to ensure standard, quality and originality.
  • Articles which are outside the Journal Scope will be rejected. The articles will be scrutinized by a Panel of Experts and the required corrections to be incorporated will be intimated to the corresponding author within two weeks from the date of submission of manuscript.
  • Originality of all the selected articles will be ensured by carrying out Plagiarism Verification before publication.
  • Article may have maximum of three authors.
  • If the article is accepted, first author has to forward their undertaking form to The Editor, JECE.
